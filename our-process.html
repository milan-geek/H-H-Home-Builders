Our Process

Process

Our Process Guarantees Quality – Each and Every Time

At H&H Home Builders, we have a comprehensive five-step home-building process. Read through it carefully, then take action to create the sustainable home of your dreams!

Step 1

Design & Budget

To start off, we work closely with you to figure out the style preferences and expected budgets. This is the phase in which you’ll choose whether to take advantage of our stunning pre-planned designs, or to opt for a fully custom-built creation instead. We’ll guide you through budgeting, drafting, concept enhancements, preliminary selections, and specification development, keeping you apprised of available options all the way through.

1
2
Step 2

Contract & Schedule

Next up, we handle the tedious yet unavoidable administrative work. H&H Home Builders aims to develop a personal relationship with every client, making sure you understand the contracts, payment structure, and projected construction schedules. Only a full understanding of these phases guarantees complete customer satisfaction. Once this is out of the way, we introduce you to our supply partners and start showing you how your dreams will come to life!

Step 3

Visualize & Build

Communication is a crucial part of our homebuilding process. As we mark, pour, and construct your sustainable home, we provide progress updates and milestone meetings at the jobsite. Our owners are present at the job sites every day, ensuring that everything is proceeding according to plan, and the highest quality of workmanship is maintained.

3
4
Step 4

Quality Control

Whenever you feel the need to visit the jobsite in person and see how things are going, we’ll direct your attention to the task list outlining the progress and the detailed specifications provided to our contractors. Our thorough quality control methods leave nothing to chance – up until the moment you’re handed the keys to your sustainable dream home, we keep a close eye on every last detail.

Step 5

Celebrate the Future

Finally, as you enjoy your sustainable green home for decades to come, we hope that you’ll consider us lifelong friends and partners. Whether it’s lowered utility bills, reduced carbon footprints, or unparalleled home comfort that makes you think of H&H Home Builders, you’ll know what we mean when we say that “the name means quality”.

H&H Home Builders have constructed Energy Star Compliant homes since we burst onto the building landscape in 1995 as ‘North Liberty’s Premier Builder’. Since then, Energy Star Guidelines have evolved into what we consider today as the pinnacle of efficiency and we, as builders of ‘green’ homes, have kept pace with those guidelines and have adapted them into each home that we build. H&H Home Builders will build you what is known today as a ‘green’ home, but what has always been lesser known as a ‘sustainable’ home. Sustainable homes are quality built so that they last not just decades, but are built to maintain value, encouraging pride of ownership for generations to come. An H&H ‘green’ Home uses raw materials in a responsible way, using fewer resources, resulting in the use of less energy.
